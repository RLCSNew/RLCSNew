RLCS Winter Split

Format:

  • Each tournament will begin with a 16-team Group Stage.
  • The Group Stage will be four groups of four teams.
  • Each match will be Best-of-5 (BO5).
  • The top three teams from each group will advance, while the fourth-place team in each group will be eliminated.
  • The remaining teams will be seeded into a 12-team Single Elimination Bracket.
  • The Round of 12 will be BO5.
  • The Quarterfinals, Semifinals, and Grand Finals will be Best-of-7 (BO7).
  • The team that claims victory in the Single-Elimination Bracket wins the tournament.
Winter Open:

The Winter Open is the first chance for RLCS teams to gain points for determining seeding for the RLCS World Championship. All teams are allowed to play in this tournament. It was held from January 27th to February 5th.

EU winners were: Karmine Corp
NA winners were: Gen.G Mobil1 Racing

Winter Cup:

After teams qualify through the open qualifers bracket, the group stage begins. 4 groups with 4 teams in each group play against each other, the winner of the group moves to the quarter finals immediately. Places 2 and 3 in the group qualify for round 1 and th place is eliminated.

EU winners were: Karmine Corp
NA winners were: Faze Clan

Winter Invitational:

Teams are invited to play in a tournament with the same format as the Winter Open, and Cup. Winners of the invitational get additional points that matter toward RLCS World Championship seeding.

EU winners were: Team Liquid
NA winners were: Faze Clan
